Boise

The Capital of Idaho

Some facts about Boise

  • Population: 235,684
  • The city was founded in 1863 and incorporated in 1864
  • Boise is located in southwestern Idaho in Ada County
  • The city is classified as urban
  • According to the 2022 Census, the median household income of Boise was $76,402 compared to the median household income of the state of Idaho which is $70,214
