Boise

The Capital of Idaho

Some facts about Boise

  • Population: 235,684
  • The city was founded in 1863 and incorporated in 1864
  • Boise is located in southwestern Idaho in Ada County
  • The city is classified as urban
  • According to the Census Bureau, the median household income of Boise is $68,373

Boise Trivia

  1. Boise is also called "The City of Trees"
  2. The statehouse is heated by an underground hot springs
  3. On Sundays it is against the law to ride a merry-go-round in Boise
