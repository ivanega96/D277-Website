Boise

The Capital of Idaho

Some facts about Boise

  • Population: 235,684 (Kiddle Encyclopedia, n.d.)
  • The city was founded in 1863 and incorporated in 1864 (Kiddle Encyclopedia, n.d.)
  • Boise is located in southwestern Idaho in Ada County (Kiddle Encyclopedia, n.d.)
  • The city is classified as urban (Kiddle Encyclopedia, n.d.)
  • According to the 2022 Census, the median household income of Boise was $76,402 compared to the median household income of the state of Idaho which is $70,214 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.)

Boise Trivia

  1. Boise is also called "The City of Trees" (Build Idaho, n.d.)
  2. The statehouse is heated by an underground hot springs (Build Idaho, n.d.)
  3. On Sundays it is against the law to ride a merry-go-round in Boise (Build Idaho, n.d.)
