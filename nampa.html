Nampa

One of Idaho's largest Cities

Some facts about Nampa

  • Population: 100,200 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.)
  • The city was founded in 1886 and incorporated in April 17, 1891 (Kiddle, n.d.a)
  • Nampa is located east of Caldwell in Canyon County
  • Nampa is classified as urban (Kiddle, n.d.a)
  • According to the 2022 Census, the median household income of Nampa was $67,346 compared to the median household income of the state of Idaho which is $70,214 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.)

Nampa Trivia

  1. It has the highest population of any city in Canyon County (Kiddle, n.d.a)
  2. The Carnegie library burned down in 1966 (Kiddle, n.d.a)
