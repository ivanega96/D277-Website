Nampa

One of Idaho's largest Cities

Some facts about Nampa

  • Population: 106,860
  • The city was founded in 1886 and incorporated in April 17, 1891
  • Nampa is located east of Caldwell in Canyon County
  • Nampa is classified as urban
  • According to the 2020 Census the median household income of Nampa was $53,205

Source: Nampa, Idaho Wiki

Nampa Trivia

  1. It has the highest population of any city in Canyon County
  2. There was a book ban in 2022 which banned books including The Kite Runner.
