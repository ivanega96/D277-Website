Meridian

A Fast Growing City

Some facts about Meridian

  • Population: 117,635
  • The city was founded in 1893 and incorporated in 1909
  • Meridian is located in Ada County in the north central part of the Treasure Valley
  • Meridian is classified as Urban
  • According to the 2022 Census, the median household income of Meridian was $93,296 compared to the median household income of the state of Idaho which is $70,214

Source: Meridian, Idaho Wiki
