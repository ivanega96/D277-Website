Meridian

A Fast Growing City

Some facts about Meridian

  • Population: 117,635 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.)
  • The city was founded in 1893 and incorporated in 1909 (Hughes Group Blog Team, 2018)
  • Meridian is located east of Boise and west of Nampa (Hughes Group Blog Team, 2018)
  • Meridian is classified as Urban (Wikipedia contributors, 2023)
  • According to the 2022 Census, the median household income of Meridian was $93,296 compared to the median household income of the state of Idaho which is $70,214 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.)

Meridian Trivia

  1. It has the 2nd highest population in Ada County (Wikipedia contributors, 2023)
  2. Meridian used to be called Hunter (Wikipedia contributors, 2023)
  3. The movie Bronco Billy had some scenes that were filmed in Meridian (Wikipedia contributors, 2023)
