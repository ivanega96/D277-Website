Some facts about Idaho

The population of Idaho is currently 1,839,106 Ada County was named after Ada Riggs, the daughter of one of the founders of Boise The state bird is the Mountain Bluebird
The state gem is the Star Garnet The state motto is the latin phrase "Esto Perpetua" which means "Be Eternal" Idaho became a state July 3rd, 1890
The state flower is the Syringa The state fruit is Wild Huckleberry The state fish is Cutthroat Trout

Source: Idaho Government - Facts & Symbols
